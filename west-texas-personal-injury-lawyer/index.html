Oil Field Accidents

The Permian Basin is one of the most active oil-producing regions in the world. This means thousands of workers face daily hazards including explosions and fires, equipment failures, falls from heights, vehicle accidents on well sites, chemical exposure, and struck-by incidents. If you've been injured in an oil field accident, you may have claims beyond workers' compensation—including third-party lawsuits against contractors, equipment manufacturers, and other companies.
