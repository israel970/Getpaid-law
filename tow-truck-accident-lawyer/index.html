Why You Need a Tow Truck Accident Lawyer

Tow truck drivers often race to accident scenes to secure business, leading to aggressive and reckless driving. These heavy vehicles, designed to haul disabled cars and trucks, can cause severe damage in collisions.

Whether you were hit by a tow truck on the highway or injured at an accident scene, you deserve compensation for your injuries. Our attorneys handle claims against tow truck operators and their insurance companies.

Types of Tow Truck Accident Cases

Flatbed Truck Accidents

Large flatbed tow trucks causing accidents while transporting vehicles.

Wrecker Collisions

Heavy-duty wreckers crashing while rushing to accident scenes.

Roadside Accidents

Tow trucks striking motorists stopped on the side of the road.

Improper Towing Accidents

Vehicles falling off tow trucks or towed vehicles breaking free.

Racing Accidents

Tow truck drivers speeding and driving aggressively to reach accidents first.

Loading Accidents

Injuries occurring while vehicles are being loaded onto tow trucks.

Proving Liability in Tow Truck Accident Cases

Establishing liability in truck accident cases requires thorough investigation and understanding of federal trucking regulations. Key elements we examine include:

  • Driver Negligence: Speeding, distracted driving, fatigue, or impairment
  • Company Negligence: Inadequate training, pressure to violate hours of service, poor maintenance
  • Regulatory Violations: FMCSA violations, overweight loads, improper cargo securement
  • Equipment Failures: Defective brakes, tires, or other mechanical components

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do tow trucks drive so aggressively?

Many tow truck drivers work on commission and race to accident scenes to secure the tow. This creates dangerous driving conditions.

Can I sue a tow truck company?

Yes, tow truck companies can be held liable for their drivers negligent actions under respondeat superior and negligent hiring theories.

What if the tow truck hit me at an accident scene?

Being struck at an accident scene while waiting for help is especially tragic. You have the right to compensation from the tow truck operator.

What injuries are common in tow truck accidents?

Due to the size and weight of tow trucks, victims often suffer serious injuries including broken bones, spinal injuries, and traumatic brain injuries.
