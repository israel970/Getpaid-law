Why Rio Grande Valley Accident Victims Trust Our Network

The Rio Grande Valley is one of the fastest-growing regions in Texas, with over 1.3 million residents in Hidalgo, Cameron, Starr, and Willacy counties. With international commerce flowing through border crossings and heavy traffic on US-83 and US-281, accidents are a daily reality. When you're injured, you need attorneys who understand Valley communities and know how to fight for fair compensation.

Serving the Rio Grande Valley

  • McAllen — Hidalgo County's largest city and regional hub
  • Brownsville — Cameron County seat at the southernmost tip of Texas
  • Harlingen — Valley International Airport and medical center
  • Edinburg — County seat and home to UTRGV
  • Mission — Growing city west of McAllen
  • Pharr — International bridge traffic and commerce
  • Weslaco — Mid-Valley community
  • San Benito, Mercedes, La Feria — Communities throughout the Valley

Dangerous Roads in the RGV

  • US-83 / Expressway 83 — Main east-west corridor connecting all Valley cities
  • US-281 — Major north-south route from the Valley to San Antonio
  • Highway 77 — Brownsville to Harlingen and beyond
  • International Boulevard — Heavy traffic near border crossings
  • 10th Street / Business 83 — Local roads with high accident rates

Unique RGV Concerns
