We're Tired of Seeing People Get Ripped Off

Too many injury victims settle for less than they deserve. We built GetPaid.law to change that.

The Problem We're Solving

Here's the ugly truth about personal injury cases: the system is stacked against you.

Insurance companies have one goal—pay you as little as possible. They have teams of adjusters trained to minimize your claim, lawyers on retainer to fight you, and billion-dollar budgets to wait you out.

And sometimes, even the attorneys who are supposed to help you are part of the problem. We've seen cases where the lawyer walks away with more money than the injured client. Cases where people are pressured into quick settlements that don't cover their medical bills. Cases where the "free consultation" turns into being ignored for months.

It's wrong. And we got tired of watching it happen.

Our Promise

Every attorney in our network is vetted. They don't just have good case results—they have a track record of treating clients right. Because winning a big verdict means nothing if the client gets screwed on the backend.

Why We Built GetPaid.law

We built GetPaid.law because finding a good personal injury lawyer shouldn't be a gamble. You're already dealing with injuries, medical bills, missed work, and stress. The last thing you need is to wonder if your attorney is actually fighting for you—or just their fee.

Our mission is simple: connect injured Texans with attorneys who will fight for maximum compensation AND treat them with respect.

We don't take referral fees that inflate your costs. We don't send you to whoever pays us the most. We match you with attorneys based on one thing: who will get you the best outcome.

You've already been through enough. Let us help you find someone who will fight like hell to make it right.
