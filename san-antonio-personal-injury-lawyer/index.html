Texas Personal Injury Law Basics

Statute of Limitations: 2 Years

Texas gives you 2 years from the date of your injury to file a lawsuit. Miss this deadline and you lose your right to sue forever.

Texas is an At-Fault State

Unlike no-fault states, Texas allows you to pursue compensation directly from the person who caused your accident.

Modified Comparative Fault

Texas follows a 51% rule. You can recover damages as long as you're 50% or less at fault for the accident. Your compensation is reduced by your percentage of fault.

No Damage Caps (Usually)

Texas doesn't cap damages in most personal injury cases. You can recover full compensation for medical bills, lost wages, pain and suffering, and more.
