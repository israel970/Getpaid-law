How to Get Paid

We make it simple. Here's exactly what happens when you reach out.

1

Tell Us What Happened

Call us or fill out the form. Tell us about your accident—when it happened, how you were injured, and what you're dealing with now. This takes about 5 minutes. There's no pressure, no obligation, and everything you share is confidential.

2

We Match You With the Right Attorney

Based on your case type, location, and specific situation, we connect you with an experienced personal injury attorney from our vetted network. Not just any attorney—the right one for your case. Someone who has handled cases like yours and knows how to win.

3

Free Consultation

Your matched attorney will reach out to discuss your case in detail. They'll explain your options, answer your questions, and give you an honest assessment of what your case is worth. This consultation is completely free—no strings attached.

4

Your Attorney Goes to Work

If you decide to move forward, your attorney handles everything: investigating the accident, gathering evidence, dealing with insurance companies, negotiating your settlement, and going to court if necessary. You focus on healing. They focus on getting you paid.

5

You Get Paid

When your case settles or wins at trial, you receive your compensation. Your attorney only gets paid if you get paid—that's how contingency fees work. No upfront costs, no hourly bills, no financial risk to you.
